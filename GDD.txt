Game Design Document for Space Invaders:

Concept: A minimalist, high-stakes space survival game where players pilot a mobile air turret on the ground that shoots invading aliens coming down from the top of the screen.

1. Game Overview
Concept: A fixed-shooter where a player-controlled cannon defends Earth against an escalating grid of descending aliens.
Target Audience: Casual arcade players.
Core Loop: Shoot aliens → Avoid enemy fire → Earn points → Advance to the next, faster wave.

2. Core Gameplay Mechanics
Player Control: Horizontal movement only (left/right). 
One projectile allowed on-screen at a time.
Enemy Behavior: 5x11 grid moves horizontally as a unit. 
Reaching a screen edge drops the grid down one row and reverses its direction.
Dynamic Speed: Enemy movement speed increases automatically as the total number of remaining aliens decreases.
Win/Loss Conditions: Win by destroying all 55 aliens. Lose instantly if aliens touch the player's ground level or if the player loses all lives.

3. Key Features & Elements
Destructible Cover: Four green bunkers shield the player. Both player and enemy lasers permanently erode them pixel by pixel.
Mystery Ship: A UFO randomly flies across the top screen edge, awarding variable bonus points if destroyed.
Audio Tension: A continuous 4-note descending bass loop that increases in tempo to match the remaining alien speed.

4. Scoring
Point Values: Top row (Squid) = 30 pts; Middle rows (Crab) = 20 pts; Bottom rows (Octopus) = 10 pts; UFO = 50 to 300 pts.
